The Case For Testosterone

On Zepbound 15mg 1+ year with no weight movement. 25–40% of weight lost on GLP-1 is lean muscle — testosterone is the primary protector. Improves insulin sensitivity and works synergistically with tirzepatide. Many menopause specialists now consider it standard of care.

"I have been on Zepbound 15mg for over a year with no weight loss response. I swim 10km+ weekly and am adding weight training. I am concerned about muscle loss and would like to discuss adding low-dose testosterone to my HRT protocol."
HRT + Skin Collagen

Optimising your oestrogen patch dose is the single most powerful skin treatment available. Oestrogen directly stimulates collagen production and skin thickness — every device treatment in Section 04 is amplified by optimal HRT.

Needle-free mesotherapy gun. No downtime. Ideal for neck, décolleté, and jaw on non-needling days.

Systemic collagen stimulation via heat shock proteins. Amplifies every topical and device treatment. 3× weekly minimum.

⚠ HA Filler Interaction — Critical

Wait minimum 4 weeks after any HA filler before applying RF heat to the same area. Your summer 2025 fillers are fully settled — you are clear to proceed. Before any future filler appointment: pause RF for 4 weeks before and after injection.

Serum Guide

Qure Wrinkle Concentrate

Primary serum for micro-infusion sessions. Full face + neck. Targets collagen cross-linking and fine line depth. 1 full vial per session.

Qure Dark Spot Concentrate

Alternate with wrinkle concentrate. Targets hyperpigmentation on cheeks and décolleté. Best infused post-RF when circulation is elevated.

EZ Injector Loading

HA booster, peptide serum, or diluted Qure concentrate. Needle-free delivery ideal for neck and décolleté platysmal area.

Post-Treatment Barrier

After any needling: pure HA serum only, then ceramide moisturiser. Zero actives for 48 hours — non-negotiable.
