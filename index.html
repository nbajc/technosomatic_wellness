The Case For Testosterone — Bring This To Your Doctor
  • On Zepbound 15mg 1+ year with no weight movement — testosterone supports fat metabolism directly
  • 25–40% of weight lost on GLP-1 drugs is lean muscle — testosterone is the primary protector against this
  • Swimming 10km+ weekly without testosterone makes muscle building extremely difficult at 56

  • Many menopause specialists now consider it standard of care, not optional

Eating Window Reset

Nutrition Protocol

The eating window is the primary block. One large meal at 4–7pm has neutralised Zepbound's effect every day for a year.

Why The Scale Has Not Moved — The Core Issue
  • Insulin sensitivity is lowest in the evening — eating at 4–7pm drives fat storage even in a deficit
  • One large meal cannot hit leucine threshold multiple times — muscle protein synthesis requires 3 separate triggers daily
  • Swimming 2.5km fasted burns muscle as fuel — offsetting fat loss and keeping scale static
  • Tirzepatide's appetite suppression is wasted all day then overwhelmed by genuine hunger at 4pm
  • This single change could unlock what a year of medication has not

Resistance Program

Training Protocol

3× weekly weight training designed for your exact profile. Muscle preservation on Zepbound is non-negotiable — this is how you keep it.

Key Principles
  • Heavy enough that last 2–3 reps of each set are genuinely difficult — if it's easy, it is not working
  • Progressive overload every session — increase weight or reps to build muscle
  • 90 second minimum rest between strength sets — do not rush
  • Lift BEFORE swimming on same days — resistance training on glycogen, swim as active recovery
  • Your aerobic base means weights will work faster than for most people starting this journey

  • Should progesterone increase to 200mg Prometrium given age and oestrogen dose?
